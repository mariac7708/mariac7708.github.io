Movie Webpage project

Context

For this project, I worked with my partner Valentino to create a fake movie page. The catagory we choose was hoorror and we choose to base it off of the Scream franchise. We decided to make our own version of it and came up with our own dialogue and designed it oursleves completely.

Content

For our project, we decided to go with a comic/movie premier theme. We used two local images to show our vision. Which was a movie poster of a scream movie and a short comic strip to give it more variation. We used an image with a black background with a blood slash as our background to show that the whole story of the movie is gorey and thrilling. We used spans and divs to enunciate and make parts of our work of our texts and work pop. We also decided to use two different fonts, one for the title and another for the sneakpeak dialogue. We also took advantage of the general use of headers, body, and paragraph texts to organize our work more neatly and assign each text something special.

Reflection

After working on the project and running into different problems, one of the biggest take aways I had was when I had to set up the spans and learn wehre it is I should use them. After getting help from a classmate, I learned that a span is used to differentiate a word from all the rest, make it stand out. With this, I decided to use the spans to make the actions in the dialogue stand out. This was how I was able to elevate my work and make it appear more interesting to the audience.
